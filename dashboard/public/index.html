Why we exist

We were the ones always asking for rides. "Can you drop me?" is a phrase with history — it's how we got to weddings, to airports, to grocery runs in a new country. Vaahana is what happens when that culture meets software.

Why not just use Uber?

We're not trying to replace it. We're a different thing altogether — for when the other thing doesn't fit.

Who's driving
Someone from your community. Verified email, linked WhatsApp, visible profile.

How you talk
WhatsApp. A platform you already trust, with a history you can see.

Vaahana doesn't price. Drivers and riders agree on cost-sharing themselves, off-platform.

Nobody — yet. We take no cut. The app is free.

Our promises

Things we're proud not to do yet.

  • 01
    We don't do background checks. Drivers are community members, not employees. We trust a verified email and a linked WhatsApp the way community has always worked.
  • 02
    We don't take a cut. There's nothing to cut. The app is free. Drivers and riders arrange any cost-sharing themselves, off-platform.
  • 03
    We don't operate in 42 cities. We're in six, today. We'll say when we're in more — city by city, community by community.
  • 04
    We don't surge price. We don't price at all.
